School Facilities

Our school is proud to provide a wide range of well-maintained and modern facilities that support and enhance the learning experience of our students.

School Bus

We offer a reliable and efficient school bus service to ensure that students can commute to and from school comfortably.

Playground

We have well-maintained outdoor spaces where students can relax, socialize, and engage in recreational activities

Healthy Canteen

Our cafeteria offers nutritious meals and a comfortable space for students to enjoy their meals and interact with peers

Positive Learning

Our school library serves as a hub for knowledge and learning. It houses a vast collection of books, including textbooks, reference materials, fiction, non-fiction, and digital resources
